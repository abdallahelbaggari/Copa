1Who We Are

Copa ("the App", "we", "us", "our") is an independent football application built for the Pi ecosystem, developed by Abdallah Elbaggari. Copa is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected to Pi Core Team, Inc. Copa operates as an independent third-party application within the Pi ecosystem.

Copa is accessible through the Pi Browser at copa-pi.pages.dev and the Pi App Studio at copa.pi.

3Pi Network Authentication

Copa currently uses the Pi Network SDK exclusively for user authentication. When you tap "Sign In with Pi", authentication occurs through Pi's official SDK. Copa receives only your Pi username from this process, used solely for display purposes within the app.

Current Implementation: Copa uses Pi.authenticate() with scopes ['username', 'payments']. No alternative authentication methods are currently available.

Pi Network's own privacy practices govern data handled through their SDK. We encourage you to review Pi Network's Privacy Policy.

4Pi Payments and Transactions

All payments within Copa are currently processed through the Pi Network SDK using Pi.createPayment(). Copa currently processes transactions in Pi only, in line with Pi ecosystem practices for Pi-exclusive applications.

7Infrastructure — Cloudflare

Copa is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare may collect standard infrastructure logs including IP addresses and request timestamps as part of their network operations. This is governed by Cloudflare's Privacy Policy. Copa does not access or process Cloudflare infrastructure logs.

8Data Retention

Copa currently does not maintain a server-side user database. Certain preferences and app state may be stored locally in your browser's localStorage and remain under your control.

To remove local Copa data: Clear site data for copa-pi.pages.dev in your Pi Browser settings. This removes locally stored preferences and app state.

Transaction records that have been confirmed on the Pi Blockchain are subject to Pi Network's blockchain properties and Pi Core Team's applicable policies.

9Service Availability

Features, content, statistics, and external data within Copa may change or become unavailable at any time without prior notice, including due to changes at third-party data providers, Pi Network infrastructure, or Cloudflare services. Copa aims to provide a reliable experience but cannot guarantee uninterrupted availability.

10Children's Privacy

Copa is not directed at individuals under the age of 13 or the minimum eligible age under applicable local law. Copa inherits Pi Network's eligibility requirements through exclusive Pi SDK authentication. If you believe a child has used Copa in violation of this policy, please contact us.
